Pricing

First sessions are free! After that, we will charge $15/session.

There are no commitments.

Compared to other standardized testing prep programs, which can charge over $1000 for all sessions or even private tutors that may charge $80 per session, we believe that we represent an inexpensive option to standardized testing or general tutoring with the additional benefit of experience.

In addition, with the completion of 5 sessions (not including the first free session), you will be eligible for free internship essay consultation by AirCollegia, LLC. Please consult their website for details.
