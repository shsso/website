About Us

We are a group of exceptional students, all in the top 5% of our class and all National Merit semifinalists, who are committed to providing rigorous tutoring services for those who are following a rigorous curriculum. We specialize in SAT/ACT tutoring and tutoring for AP and honors classes, and we guarantee you excellent service. We believe that our scores and grades are proof of our expertise, and our flexible hours and manageable rates will keep you with us month after month. We are so confident that you'll stay with us that your first session with any tutor is free. And, for a limited time, AirCollegia is offering free internship essay guidance to anybody who stays with us for a month (5 paid, 1-hour sessions). 100% of our proceeds go towards funding the Solon High School Science Olympiad team.

Our wonderful staff is composed of Sonia Godbole, Nick Palumbo, Eugene Rabinovich, Rebecca Rabinovich, Stephanie Sang, Daniel Sperling, and Lillian Sun. Feel free to check out our staff page for specific information about each of them.
