Plans

Our plans are very customizable - tell us what YOU need. Our default route is 1 session/week for 1-2 hours, but again, less or more may be requested. We will meet at a location of your choice.

Standardized Testing

Suggested Materials: Official SAT Guide/any other practice book

We can supply our own practice test books for you to use, but keep in mind that they may be already marked with answers. It is more convenient for you to purchase your own copy.

At the first session, you will take a diagnostic test. From there, we will determine your weaknesses and strengths and set a lesson plan. It would be helpful if you could indicate how many sessions you plan to attend to give us a scope of the amount of time that we have to help you succeed.

Advanced-level Course Tutoring (Honors/AP)

Due to the very specific nature of this tutoring, there is no set curriculum. Instead, we will talk with you at your first session and determine what factor is your weakness (study habits, lack of knowledge of certain subjects, etc.)
