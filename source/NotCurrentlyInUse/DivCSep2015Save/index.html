Latest Team News and Events


- May 23rd, 2012 -

Our team defended our title and won 1st place at the National Tournament again! With a score of 158 points, we have set a Division C record for lowest amount of points ever. Our Solon Division B team won the National championship for the 5th year in a row, setting a Division B record with 200 points. Congratulations to Centerville High School (4th place) and Magsig Middle School (9th place). Thank you to everyone who made this national title possible. Congratulations to everyone on an amazing (and undefeated) year!

- June 8, 2011 -

Congratulations to Roger Zou and Yuxi Liu, our new captains for the 2011-2012 Season!

What new wonders, undreamt of in our past, will become wrought in another season? and another? How far will our great team have wandered by the end of next year? And the next decade? Our extraordinary alumni, arrayed on many nations and beyond, will be unified by their common heritage, by their laggard for their second home in humble Room 240, and by the knowledge that whatever Science Olympiad may be, the only SOLON Science Olympians in all the universe, come from here.

Infinite thanks to our captains, Eugene Rabinovich and Stephanie Sang, and the rest of the graduating seniors for helping to make last season the most memorable and successful yet!

We will be recognized June 13th by the Solon Board of Education, and June 15th by the Ohio House of Representatives in Columbus for our tremendous success last season.

- May 29th, 2011 -

We are extremely appreciative of our community's support! On June 13th, we will be attending the Solon Board Meeting (which will be featured on the local Solon network) and on June 15th, we will be traveling down to Columbus to meet with our state's legislators. Thank you to The Plain Dealer, The Solon Times, and the Chagrin Solon Sun for featuring us. - May 23rd, 2011 -

Our team captured 1st place at the National Tournament, a feat that hasn't been achieved since 1998. Our Solon Division B team won the National championship for the 4th year in a row! Congratulations to Centerville High School (2nd place) and Magsig Middle School (5th place)! Thank you, thank you, and thank you to everyone who made this dream worth fighting for possible.

- May 16th, 2011 -

We leave for Nationals in two days! We hope to continue with our tradition of excellence and represent Ohio with the utmost glory! Bakaw!

- May 2nd, 2011 -

This past weekend, we placed 2nd in the State, trailing by 16 points from the State champions, qualifying for the National Tournament in Madison, Wisconsin! Congratulations to the Division B team who broke a new State record with only 69 points! We are very grateful and appreciate any alums who have helped us on our way.

- August 21, 2010 -

- July 25, 2010 -

Despite facing the drudges of antagonistic weather, we managed to draw from our courage and bravery of heart and raise $70 during today's car wash. The forces of nature put up an admirable stance; at some points, persistent drizzle and ominous, darkly-shrouded clouds threatened to dispel our spirits. However, that could not stop Solon Science Olympiad's determination. With sponges and hoses in hand, we prevailed. We shall carry this attitude throughout the season.
