4/18/10 STATE CHAMPIONS!! Solon's 15 top competitors and 5 alternates took a resounding first place at the Ohio Science Olympiad State Tournament, tying Centerville's long-standing record of a mere 78 points at the competition. Additional congratulations to the Solon Middle School, who finished first in their own division. We look forward to representing Ohio along with Centerville at the National Tournament at the University of Illinois, Urbana Champaign.
